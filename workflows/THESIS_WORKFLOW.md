# Thesis Workflow

This workflow is not usable until Phase 3 entry gates in `governance/RESEARCH_SCOPE.md` are satisfied.

## Phase 3 Entry Gates (all required)

- At least 3 completed Historical Revolution Research assets (`REV-HIST-###`)
- At least 5 cross-cycle validated Patterns (`PAT-###`)
- At least 3 Historical Mappings (`MAP-###`)
- At least 1 completed Monthly Review (`MR-YYYY-MM`)
- A stable, repeatable Phase 2 workflow, evidenced by running it more than once without redesign

No agent may create or act on a `THS-###` thesis asset until these gates are confirmed open in `governance/WORK_BOARD.md`.

## When Unlocked: Requirements for Any Thesis

Per Gate 5 in `workflows/RESEARCH_WORKFLOW.md`, a thesis (`templates/THESIS_TEMPLATE.md`) is eligible only if it has:

- a credible historical mechanism, traceable to a stable historical research asset and at least one reviewed mapping
- current AI-era evidence
- a defined investable universe
- falsification metrics

## Mandatory Prediction Journal Entry

Every thesis, on creation, must be accompanied by a prediction journal entry (`templates/PREDICTION_TEMPLATE.md`) recording: hypothesis, reasoning chain, historical basis, validation metrics, failure conditions, review date. A thesis without a prediction journal entry is incomplete and must not be marked `active`.

## Exception

A thesis candidate may be drafted and explicitly labeled as pending its own mapping's review (see `governance/HIS-001_RESEARCH_BRIEF.md` §9F). It remains in `draft` status and confers no investment authority until Phase 3 gates open and Gate 5 passes.
